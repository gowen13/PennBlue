Welcome to Penn's Blue Memory Game

In order to beat the game you must follow the rules

Rules:

  1. 1.You will be shown the position of the cords briefly and then they will be flipped
  2. You have to match the pairs together within the time limit.
  3. Your points will be calculated from the correct pairs found and the nuber of incorrect pairs.
  4. You will be moved to the next level if you get enough points.
